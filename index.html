About Book Buddies Lounge

Building Rwanda's future through reading and community connection

Hi! I'm Chanelle Imena, and I'm passionate about transforming how children in rural Rwanda experience reading. Through my ENT 110 project, I've embarked on an incredible journey to address a critical challenge that I believe holds the key to unlocking Rwanda's future potential.

I discovered that children in rural Rwanda have limited access to engaging books outside their formal education, and reading for personal development is often viewed as non-essential. This realization sparked my mission to create something extraordinary: Book Buddies Lounge - community reading spaces that transform reading from a neglected activity into an exciting, culturally-valued experience.

üéØ Our Mission

Give every child aged 6-14 the opportunity to explore storybooks and non-fiction through fun, social activities that build the critical thinking and creativity skills essential for Rwanda's future.

What Makes Us Different:

  • üìö Social Reading: We turn reading into a community activity, not solitary homework
  • üåç Cultural Integration: We celebrate both Rwandan traditions and global perspectives

  • ü§ù Community Ownership: Local volunteers and families are partners, not just participants
  • üß† Skills Development: Focus on critical thinking, creativity, and communication
